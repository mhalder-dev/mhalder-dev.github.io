GATE 01CHECK-IN · ABOUTNow boarding

Engineer by day, researcher by design.

At Akij iBOS, work centers on the supplier side of Travilo — a multi-tenant SaaS flight-booking platform. That means connecting airline and GDS systems such as Amadeus, Sabre, AirArabia, AirBlue, Tripjack, and Travelport, each with its own protocol, and folding them behind one clean .NET service layer over REST, gRPC, and SOAP.

The other half is research. Four peer-reviewed papers in deep learning and computer vision, a BSc thesis on hybrid deep learning for pedestrian trajectory imputation, and a year teaching computer science as a university lecturer. The engineering keeps the research grounded; the research keeps the engineering curious.

Now looking for backend engineering roles, research collaborations, and PhD or lecturer opportunities in machine learning and computer vision.
